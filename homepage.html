Why Specialized E-Waste Recycling?

Electronics contain hazardous materials like mercury, cadmium, and lithium. These harm the environment if dumped into regular trash. Responsible recycling protects nature and your data.

Why Is It Free?

We fund our work by repairing, reusing, and reselling viable components. What can’t be reused is responsibly recycled with certified partners.

How We Recycle & Handle Data

  • ✔️ Devices are inspected and sorted
  • ✔️ Drives are securely wiped or removed on-site
  • ✔️ Usable components are repaired or reused
  • ✔️ Non-usable parts are recycled properly
